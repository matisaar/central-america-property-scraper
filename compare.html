🎯 Who Should Invest Where?

Costa Rica is best if you…

  • Want the strongest tourism demand (3M+ visitors/yr)
  • Want excellent healthcare (CAJA public system covers residents)
  • Want lowest closing costs (3.5–4.5%)
  • Don't mind learning some Spanish
  • Value safety — most stable country in Central America
  • Want a yoga/surf/wellness-themed rental property

Panama is best if you…

  • Want zero currency risk (USD economy)
  • Want zero foreign income tax (territorial system)
  • Are retiring and want the world's best retirement visa (Pensionado)
  • Want property tax holidays on new construction
  • Want a modern city option (Panama City skyline)
  • Want to set up a corporation for asset protection

Belize is best if you…

  • Want to do everything in English (only English-speaking CA country)
  • Are 45+ and want tax-free retirement (QRP program)
  • Want Caribbean island property (barrier reef, cayes)
  • Want the cheapest entry price ($50K+)
  • Want hands-off management (English property managers)
  • Want a ground-floor emerging market (Hopkins, San Ignacio)

🏆 Our Pick for Most Canadians: Panama

USD economy eliminates currency risk, zero foreign income tax, world-class retirement visa, and the best tax incentives for new construction. If you speak some Spanish and want maximum tourism demand, Costa Rica. If you want English and Caribbean charm, Belize.

Pro tip: Many experienced investors hold properties in 2 countries — a city/corporate rental in Panama (USD income) and a vacation rental in Costa Rica or Belize (high-season premiums). Diversification across markets reduces risk.
