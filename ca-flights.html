✈️ Best Value: YYZ → SJO on WestJet (seasonal)

Direct flights from $450 CAD round-trip in shoulder season (Nov, Apr). Book 6–8 weeks ahead for best prices. For year-round direct access, Copa to PTY is the most reliable choice.

💡 Booking Tips for Canadians

  • Shoulder season (Nov & Apr) — cheapest flights, still great weather
  • Book 6–8 weeks ahead for best prices on direct routes
  • Google Flights price alerts — set alerts for your preferred routes
  • Aeroplan / Copa connection — Copa is Star Alliance, earn Aeroplan on all Copa flights
  • Multi-city hack — fly into SJO, bus to LIR, fly home from LIR (or vice versa) to see more
  • Caribbean side flights — for Bocas del Toro, consider flying YYZ→PTY→BOC on Copa
