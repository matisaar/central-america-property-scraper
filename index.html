🌴 Why Central America for Investment?

🇨🇷 Costa Rica: Stable democracy, no army, universal healthcare. Foreigners can own property with same rights as locals. 13% VAT on rentals, property tax just 0.25%/yr. Pensionado visa available with $1k/mo income.

🇵🇦 Panama: Uses USD — no currency risk. Territorial tax system means foreign income is tax-free. Friendly Nations Visa for Canadians. Property tax exemptions for new construction (up to 20 years). Strong banking sector.

🇧🇿 Belize: English-speaking, common law system. QRP (Qualified Retired Persons) program: tax-free living with $2k/mo income. No capital gains tax. Caribbean beaches + barrier reef. Lower price point than CR/PA.
